# Favicon Instructions

1. Replace `public/favicon.ico` with your own icon (32x32 or 48x48, .ico or .png).
2. You can use any favicon generator online to create a .ico file from your logo.
3. Once replaced, commit and push to update the favicon on your site.

**Tip:** Browsers cache favicons. After updating, hard-refresh (Cmd+Shift+R) to see changes.
